About

The news you have seen in the home page are fake, and these kind of fake news are everywhere. In the past five years, the internet has spread all over the world, and the speed of information transmitted through the internet is unimaginable. At the same time that the development of the Internet has become faster, journalism is also expanding its own development. In the past, the news only appears on TV or in newspapers, and nowadays when we turn on our phones, we can see countless kinds of news. These features have brought convenience to our lives and also changed people's perception. Faced with the development trend of the news industry, fake news has begun to emerge quietly in the news media, making true news begin to be affected and questioned. Authenticity is the life of news, and any report that violates the facts or is inconsistent with the facts can be called fake news. On social media, everyone is free to express their opinions, and some people can pretend to be fake news accounts to publish fake news. And before this news is not proven to be fake news, people will express their opinions. Whether positive or negative.



Fake news causes citizens to lose trust in social media. The rapid development of the self-media community has not only increased the influence of fake news but also accelerated the spread of fake news. Although I don't know their true purpose, this wrong information seriously affected the authority and credibility of the news media; led to the appearance of wrong public opinion; violated the rights of citizens; wasted time and money, and caused a huge impact on society, It can also cause serious misunderstandings and contradictions between countries.
